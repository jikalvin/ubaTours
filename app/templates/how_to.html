Apply For Admission

August 3, 2021 by Alvin

  1. Go to the school's website uniba.cm
  2. Go to the admissions dropdown
  3. Click on create account
  4. Fill the information required and submit
  5. Choose the school/faculty you are applying for and also choose the program you wish to apply for
  6. Pay platform and registration charges and wait for your admission letter

Create Student Account

  1. Go to the schools website uniba.cm
  2. Under the admissions dropdown select check admission list
  3. Search for the uba student online platform and on the search results select "create student account"
  4. Fill in the information required and submit

Pay School Fees

  1. Go to ubastudent.online
  2. Click on the "pay fees tab"

Register For Courses

  1. Go to ubastudent.online
  2. Click on the "course registration tab"
